2. What are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that the sites visited by the user send to their terminal, where they are stored to be then retransmitted to the same sites at the next visit of the same user. During browsing a site, the user can also receive cookies from different sites or web servers (so-called "third-party" cookies).

3. Types of Cookies used

  • Technical Cookies: Necessary for the proper functioning of the site and to provide the services requested by the user.
  • Analytical Cookies (Google Analytics): Used to collect information, in an aggregated form, on the number of users and how they visit the site itself in order to improve its performance.
  • Third-Party Cookies: The site integrates third-party functions (such as Google Maps or Swiper.js) that could install additional cookies.

4. Cookie Management

The user can manage cookie preferences directly within their browser and prevent – for example – third parties from installing them. Through browser preferences, it is also possible to delete cookies installed in the past.
