San Teodoro Apartment

A sanctuary where the sky meets the sea.

Located in a charming, recently built residential complex in San Teodoro, our three-room apartment sleeps up to 4 guests. It serves as a refined home base for those wishing to explore the crystal-clear waters of Cala Brandinchi and Lu Impostu.

The space features a dining area with a traditional Sardinian feel, a master suite, and a versatile second bedroom. Every corner is curated to provide a peaceful Mediterranean atmosphere, complete with a private veranda for your evening aperitifs.
